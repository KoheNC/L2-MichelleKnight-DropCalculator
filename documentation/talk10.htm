GM Talkthrough - Server Statistics

The server statistics cover various rates and statistics about the gameserver and the state it is in.

The statistics range from the reports available by telnet from the gameserver to visual representations of where both your entire world population are, and your current on-line population (or, at least where they were five minutes ago!)

It will also give you details on the zones from which the server is running. From underwater to fishing, they are represented by squares defined by red crosses on the corners, and blue crosses half way on each side.
