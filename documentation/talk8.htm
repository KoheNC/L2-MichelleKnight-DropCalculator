GM Talkthrough - Mob searches

Monster searches are generally no problem. The level of detail is prety much the same whomever is looking at the search result, with the exception that players who do not have map access do not see either the crosses on the thumbnail map, nor do they get the larger location map, as shown.

The drop calc comes with maps that are C5 ready. There are two directories in the images directory, one for C4 and one for C5 maps; simply copy their two files in to the images directory and you are up and running. Copy, rather than move, in case you need to revert back.

This drop calc is working for the version which notices the spawns of day and night mobs as well as permanent ones. If used on an older gameserver that only has permanent spawns, then the drop calc will adjust itself for this and although it will still show the day and night spawns as 0, it will accurately show the permanent spawns.

The spawn engine is clever in the way that for some spawns, the database has a number of different spawn points (say 10) but will only spawn mobs on (say 5) of them. That shows up in the drop calc as 5/10.

Obviously, there are no special options here, except for the fact that the percentages listed here are always the server adjusted percentages ... and for GM's and admins, there is a little button just below the thumbnail map. This is the button that gives you access to control the drops and spoils for the mob, rather than by the item. The controls are exactly the same, except the way they are listed. Some people find this the more preferable way to adjust their spawns and drops.
