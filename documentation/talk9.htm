GM Talkthrough - Account Adjustment

The accounts can be adjusted by an Admin. It is here that map or recipe calculator access can be extended to 31 or 366 days (from the current date) by simply clicking the button, although the number can be changed in either of the boxes if you wish to give a different numbers of days, for example a five day free trial of the ability to see mob locations or use the recipe calculator. It is also possible for the admin to give permanent access (well, at least until 2017 or thereabouts) to either function, or revoke rights to either of the functions.

The admin can also change the boxing status of the account (if it has been watched and is genuinely someone not causing trouble, or multiple people in an Internet Cafe, for example) and also the warning status of the account. The numbers mean nothing to the system other than a warning to other GM's that the account owner has been warned for breaching server rules.

It also allows the admin to change the access rights of the account. The drop calcs access rights are listed below the button, for reference purposes.

The admin also has a delete button, which will permanently remove the account, all characters, items and pets from the system.

NOTE - Only Admins can ban GM accounts.
